About Us

Company Name (USA): S&H Global Trade Corp.

Business Description: Our business involves selecting products, business items, and services that clients request or that S&H deems suitable, importing them into the U.S., and supplying them to clients (distributors, wholesalers, specialized sales companies, vendors with contracts with large shopping malls).

Year of Establishment: 2014

Employees: 14

Expansion Strategy

  • Strategically select and manage overseas import partners.
  • Establish and operate a system to respond immediately to sales clients' requests.

Services

  • Securing Reliable Overseas Clients: To maintain and develop corporate identity, it is crucial to secure deep relationships with reliable overseas manufacturing and export companies.
  • Securing Favorable Trade Conditions: We ensure appropriate import prices and offer competitive prices to wholesale, distribution, specialized sales companies, and supply organizations, maintaining stable trading partnerships.
  • Ensuring Client Profit Generation: We secure stable import prices to keep supply costs low for our clients, thereby ensuring their business profitability.
  • Ensuring Quality Assurance for Overseas Purchased Products: We secure quality assurance for purchased products from overseas import partners, maintaining stable sales channels and ultimately ensuring reliability with clients and suppliers at each distribution stage.

Organization

Management, Sourcing, Purchasing & Import, Import Customs Clearance, Client Collaboration, Accounting, Consulting, Sales, Distribution, Marketing

Our Strengths

1. Company Advantages

  • Overseas trading companies contracted with S&H Global Trade Corp. gain immediate and easy access to the direct-to-consumer market in the U.S.
  • Export companies can directly verify product evaluations in the U.S. market.
  • We fully serve as an intermediary for overseas export and sales companies entering the U.S. market.

2. U.S. Distribution Channel Supply Chain

S&H Global Trade Corp. has established a supply chain capable of immediately procuring all products salable in the U.S. from overseas, as large distribution chain supply partners are our clients. This enables stable business partnerships for overseas product suppliers.

3. Production System Setup & Consulting

We can provide suitable collaborating companies for overseas sales and supply companies to establish production systems in the U.S., and we also offer business consulting for this purpose.

Company Activities

As the U.S. administration's protectionist trade policy is expected to weaken business competitiveness in importing products from overseas companies, we are striving to help promising overseas technology start-up companies enter the U.S. We are focusing on new business areas to collaborate and joint venture with U.S. companies and start-ups in the U.S.

  • Utilize existing ties with local governments in the U.S. to attract overseas companies.
  • Actively promote the information of overseas companies to members of economic organizations (e.g., chambers of commerce) in the U.S. to establish friendly relations with overseas companies.

Vision

S&H Global Trade Corp. aims to develop the distribution of new industrial products and grow into a major transaction organizer in the local public sector. We also aim to grow into a central business operator in the distribution industry, engaging clients to preempt and lead the market.

Values

Our company believes in the unchanging fundamentals of the U.S. final consumer retail market. Quality and price are key factors in satisfying consumers, and staying ahead of consumer trends is crucial. We also pay close attention to communicating product changes to consumers. We will maintain a special yet everyday trading relationship with clients who have access to the final consumer market and will further grow their unique business position.
